Corona Virus (Covid-19 Pandemic)

	This Virus was declared a Pandemic in March 11 2020  by the World Health Organization  as this virus (covid-19) can spread to contact,
   through air within  radius of 1 meter. Coronavirus first outbreak in Wuhan,China. It was found  that it could spread through the contact or 
due to any contact with infected thing and could transfer from Human to Human.

	The Symptoms, if a person is a covid (+ve) is having sore throat, high fever,loss of taste and smell and having breathing difficulties.

	This  virus first originated in china and  then it spread out throughout the globe . America became the most infected country of Coronavirus.
More & more people were dying due to coronavirus and affected the whole world so it  enforce the government to impose a lockdown in the countries and trying
 to protect the people from covid-19.

	Currently, there is no vaccine available to cure this virus and only way to be prevent ourselves  from the virus is 
washing hands properly and frequently,have a mask on to cover your nose and mouth to avoid infection entering your body.
social distancing is the most important thing during this pandemic to be safe.
